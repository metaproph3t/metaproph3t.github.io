The Meta-DAO Project

About

I am an anonymous developer building on the Solana blockchain. Before anonymity, I worked for some prominent DeFi DAOs on Ethereum. Before that, I was a software engineer in web2.

My profile picture, generated by Stable Diffusion

I'm pretty sick of the way organizations work today. Too much empire-building and image-managing, not enough work being done. I didn't want to be a part of that anymore, so I started researching and thinking about alternative forms of organizing.

The result of this navel-gazing is the Meta-DAO. The Meta-DAO will be an experimental organization, organized as a DAO on Solana. It will use futarchy, along with some other novel mechanisms, to make decisions.

As you might expect from the name, the Meta-DAO will be comprised of many member DAOs, each of which manages a single product or service. At the start, these will be Solana DeFi applications. I'm going to build an ETF-like product as a member. If you're really cool, you might even consider building your next DeFi project as a member!
